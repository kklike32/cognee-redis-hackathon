## Insights flagged for analyst review

1. **Deel pricing pressure is the #1 loss driver.** 12 deals lost on Standard tier comparison. Worth a pricing strategy review.
2. **Rippling bundle creating uncompetable deal shape.** 9 losses to Rippling on full HR+IT+Finance scope. We will never close these, but worth a "specialist scope" play to win the EOR layer alongside Rippling.
3. **Investor portfolio standardization is real.** 8 losses tied to investor defaults. Build a portco tracking list for VCs heavily standardized on Deel.
4. **Compliance scar pattern.** 11 wins driven by CFOs or compliance officers with prior audit scars. Worth seeding more case studies into the sales process explicitly aimed at "I have been here before" buyers.
5. **Remote making moves in seed contractor segment.** 4 of 5 losses to Remote were sub-30 employee, contractor-only. Promotional pricing is the lever. Re-engagement strategy for EOR conversion at 12 months.
6. **Healthtech and insurtech win rate strong.** 7 of 11 closed regulated sector deals went our way. Lean into the compliance officer buyer in these verticals.
