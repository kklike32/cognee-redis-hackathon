# G2 Review: Deel

**Source:** G2.com (SYNTHETIC for hackathon demo)
**Date posted:** 2026-05-08
**Reviewer role:** Mid Market HR Operations Lead
**Company size:** 200 employees
**Industry:** Fintech
**Rating:** 3 / 5 stars
**Review title:** "Great onboarding, frustrating support past month 6"

---

## What do you like best about Deel?

Onboarding was incredibly fast. We had our first international hire (UK) live within 36 hours. The product UX is genuinely best in class, better than every HR tool I've used. The Slack integration for support requests works.

## What do you dislike about Deel?

After about 6 months the support quality dropped noticeably. Our original CSM left and the replacement didn't know our setup. We had a contractor reclassification question in France and the answer we got was "let us check with the local entity partner". Three weeks later we were still waiting.

Transfer fees on bank withdrawals are higher than what was disclosed during sales. I found out we'd paid an extra $4,200 in transfer fees over six months that nobody flagged at the time of contract.

The new crypto wallet option for payouts is confusing for our finance team. Our CFO does not want crypto exposure on the books and it took us 15 minutes to figure out how to disable it across all employees.

## What problems is Deel solving?

Global employment compliance and payment, primarily for contractors and 4 EOR hires across UK, Spain, Germany, and Singapore.

## Recommendations to others considering Deel?

It depends on size. Under 50 people you'll love it. Over 200 people with multiple regulated jurisdictions, audit the answer to "who carries reclassification risk" before signing.
