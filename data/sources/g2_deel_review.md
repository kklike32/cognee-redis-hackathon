- Date: 2026-03-18
- Competitor: Deel
- Reviewer role: People operations lead

## Review
Deel helped our team move quickly when we needed contractor onboarding in multiple countries. The interface was easy to start with, and the vendor is well known enough that leadership felt comfortable putting it on the shortlist.

The harder part was understanding the package as we expanded from contractors to full-time employees. We had to ask several times which services were included, which were add-ons, and who would help us interpret local employment questions once we moved beyond the initial onboarding workflow.

## Competitive Signal
Deel's speed and familiarity are strengths. Packaging clarity and the support model for deeper employment questions are areas where Oyster can press with discovery.
