## What was announced

Deel launched **Compliance Copilot**, an AI agent that automatically flags compliance risks in contractor classifications across 110+ jurisdictions. The agent scans contract terms, payment patterns, and engagement structures in real time and surfaces flags before they become liabilities.

Key claims from the launch post:
- Reviews every contractor relationship against local labor law every 30 days
- Surfaces reclassification risk with country specific reasoning
- Provides a "compliance score" per worker that customers can show auditors
- Available to all Deel EOR and Contractor customers at no additional cost as of June 1, 2026

## Why this matters

This launch attacks Oyster's primary differentiation: compliance depth and proactive classification review. If Deel can deliver on the marketing claims, the "we do classification review you cannot get anywhere else" position weakens significantly.
