## 1. Identity
- **Company:** Oyster HR
- **Founded:** 2020
- **HQ:** Distributed (Charlotte NC and London anchored)
- **Funding stage:** Series C (~$150M raised, 2022)
- **ARR:** ~$100M (2026 estimate)
- **Headcount:** ~500 to 700 globally
- **Mission:** Build a more equal world by making it easy for companies to hire and grow distributed teams.
- **Status:** B Corp certified, distributed first.

## 2. Product

Global Employment Platform (EOR + Contractor + Payroll) for distributed teams.

**Core SKUs (2026):**
- **Contractor:** $29 per contractor per month. Onboard, pay, and stay compliant with international contractors.
- **Global Payroll:** $50 per user per month. Run payroll for entities you already own.
- **Employer of Record (EOR):** From $699 per employee per month. Hire full time employees in countries where you do not have a legal entity.
- **Scale (Enterprise):** Custom pricing. Multi product bundle for 200+ employee customers.

**Coverage:** 180+ countries. Owned and partner entity blend.

**Onboarding:** Average 48 hours from contract sent to employee active. (Source: Internal CSM data, 2026-Q1)

**Support model:** Named CSM + named compliance partner per customer. Ticket first with 24 hour response and 72 hour resolution targets. Not 24/7 multi channel live chat. This is intentional, see Section 4.

## 3. ICP (current, in scope for Sherlock)

- **Geography:** US headquartered companies hiring internationally
- **Stage:** Seed to Series B startups (primary: 50 to 300 employees, secondary: 300 to 800)
- **Sector:** SaaS, fintech, regulated tech (HR tech, insurtech, healthcare tech), services
- **Trigger events:** first international hire, regulatory audit failure, EU/UK expansion, multi entity simplification, CFO compliance scar from prior role

**Not our ICP:**
- Generic high growth startups without compliance complexity
- Single country teams
- 1000+ employee enterprises (different scale and procurement motion)
- Pure contractor only motions (commoditized, race to bottom)

## 4. Positioning

**Headline:** The Global Employment Platform for compliance heavy, distributed teams.

**One liner:** Hire in 180 countries with a partner who treats compliance as a feature, not a footnote.

**Three pillar value prop:**
1. **Country breadth that matters.** 180+ countries, including emerging markets where competitors push you to partner entities.
2. **Compliance depth.** Our compliance team writes contracts country by country. We are the EOR that flags reclassification risk before it becomes a tax liability.
3. **Dedicated relationships.** Named CSM and named compliance partner, not a 24/7 ticket queue.

**Brand story:** B Corp, mission driven, distributed first. We sell to teams who value how we work as much as what we do.

## 5. Where we win (segment and trigger)

- Customers expanding into EU/UK with worker classification complexity
- High compliance sectors (HR tech, insurtech, healthcare tech, regulated SaaS)
- Mid market teams (50 to 300 employees) wanting white glove onboarding
- Teams burned by a reclassification audit at scale
- Mission aligned customers who value our B Corp brand
- Customers where the CFO is in the deal (risk lens, not feature lens)

## 6. Where we lose (be honest)

- Price driven Series A buyers under 50 employees who treat EOR as commodity
- Customers needing 24/7 multi channel live support (we are ticket first by design)
- Pure contractor only motions (Deel and Remote price aggressively here)
- Enterprises wanting bundled HRIS + IT + Equity (Rippling and Deel have more bundle breadth)
- Deals already standardized on Deel by the investor's portfolio
